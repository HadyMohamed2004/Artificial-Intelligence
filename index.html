About us

Heliopolis Artificial Intelligence is a robotics team dedicated to exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics. Our team is comprised of students from different schools in Heliopolis, Egypt, who are passionate about robotics and are committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in this exciting field. With a focus on innovation and creativity, we are constantly working to develop new technologies and solutions that can make a real impact on people's lives. Our team has competed in several robotics competitions, including the FIRST LEGO League Challenge, and we are always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow as a team.
